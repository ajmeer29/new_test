Cinematography Course with 100% Job Guarantee

Cinematography course is the art of photographing and visually telling a story in a film or television production. It involves such techniques as the general composition of a scene; the lighting of the set or location; the choice of cameras, lenses, filters, and film stock; the camera angle and movements; and the integration of any special effects like lighting, framing, depth of field, zoom, focus, colour, exposure, and filtration.
Students will be taught how to make confident decisions on cameras, lenses, filters, the exposure triangle, camera angles, camera movements, and the integration of any special effects that may be necessary with the camera.
Students in the Cinematography programme are exposed to live film shoots and have the option to participate in a variety of independent and commercial projects.
